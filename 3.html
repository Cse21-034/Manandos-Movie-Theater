Double Cross

Show time: 20:00 pm

Location:Manandos at masa

best movie ever

Erica and Eric Cross two siblings known as the "Wonder Twins" are on a quest to save the women in their neighborhood from a sudden rise of sex trafficking ring that's taken over the neighborhood, including Dr. Erica's hospital emergency room and the streets run by "The Heights" Kingpin, Eric. Haunted by their past, a caring and prominent emergency physician and her brother, a notorious drug dealer, decide to take matters into their own hands by going down a path of their unique brand of vigilante justice.
